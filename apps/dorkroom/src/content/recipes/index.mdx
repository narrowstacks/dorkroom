# Development Recipes

A collection of proven film and developer combinations with detailed processing information.

## Recipe Database

Each recipe includes:

- Film and developer pairing
- Shooting ISO and push/pull information
- Development time and temperature
- Dilution ratios
- Agitation methods and schedules
- Tags for easy filtering
- Source information and community notes

Recipes are organized by film stock and can be filtered by various parameters to find the perfect development process for your needs.

Browse individual recipes using the sidebar or visit the [Development Recipes](/development) page for advanced filtering and search capabilities.
